## Per-AC verification method
- Palette: visual inspect at Phase 2 entry; count distinct body tones across segments.
- Head distinguishability: visual; eye dot or notch present.
- Field ration cadence + value: time the spawn, eat one, verify +5 score and +1 length.
- Despawn after 8 s: ignore one, verify it vanishes.
- Phase 1 regression: drive into wall, verify PATROL LOST; collect cache, verify +1 score.
- Scavenger pull: approach a cache without heading directly at it, watch it drift.
- Banners: visual at score 50 and at score 150.
- At most one ration on screen: spawn one (don't pick it up), wait, verify a second doesn't appear until first is gone.
