# P2-04 — Score multiplier bonus (2× for 10 s, cadence 60–90 s)

## Summary
Phase-2-onward bonus pickup that activates instantly on pickup (not held). Spawn cadence: uniform random 60–90 s starting at Phase 2 entry. Lifetime on field: 10 s. On pickup, all subsequent supply-cache and field-ration score increments are doubled for 10 seconds. HUD shows an active-multiplier icon with a countdown of remaining seconds during the active period.

Per PRD §10.1 cadence; §10.2 spawn rules; §10.3 (Timed effect, active immediately); §13.4 (HUD shows active multiplier).

## Persona
Primary: End User (a clear power-up moment).

## Acceptance Criteria
### End User
- [ ] A score-multiplier pickup spawns within ~90 s of Phase 2 entry.
- [ ] On pickup, the next 10 s of supply-cache and field-ration scores are doubled (cache: +2, ration: +10).
- [ ] HUD shows the multiplier as active with a visible countdown (icon + remaining seconds).
- [ ] After 10 s, scoring returns to normal.
- [ ] At most one multiplier on-field at a time; pickups overlap fine (a fresh pickup during an active effect resets the countdown to 10 s — see System spec below).

### System
- [ ] Cadence interval uniform random in [60000, 90000] ms.
- [ ] On-field lifetime is 10 s; uncollected pickups despawn.
- [ ] Active duration is 10 s from pickup time; if picked up while already active, RESET the countdown to 10 s from new pickup time (do not stack durations).
- [ ] Spawn rejection skips cells occupied by patrol, supply cache, field rations, and (eventually) bunkers + other bonuses.

## Patterns to Reuse
- Same time-driven scheduler pattern as field rations (P2-02).
- Active-effect countdown: a single timestamp `until`; rendering subtracts `(until - now) / 1000` to display seconds.

## Testing Strategy
- Manual: fudge cadence to 5 s for testing, pick up multiplier, collect a supply cache, verify +2 score. Wait 10 s, collect another, verify +1.
- Manual: pickup multiplier, then pick up another mid-effect — verify countdown resets to 10 s (no stacking).
- Regression: cache + ration scoring still correct when multiplier is inactive.
